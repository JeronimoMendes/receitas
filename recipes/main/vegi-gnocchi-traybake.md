# Vegi Gnocchi Traybake

## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 2 garlic cloves
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1½ tbsp harissa
- 1 x 400g can cooked puy lentils
- 400g cherry tomatoes
- 1 aubergine
- 1 red onion
- 500g gnocchi
- 150g mixed olives
- 200g feta
- handful parsley
- handful fresh mint
- salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. Make the dressing: whisk together lemon zest, half the lemon juice, grated garlic, olive oil, oregano, harissa, salt and pepper.
3. Halve tomatoes and dice aubergine into 2cm cubes. Toss on a baking tray with olive oil, salt and pepper. Roast for 15 minutes.
4. Thinly slice the red onion and mix with remaining lemon juice and salt. Set aside to pickle.
5. Add drained lentils, gnocchi and olives to the roasted vegetables. Pour over harissa dressing and mix well. Bake for 10 minutes, then crumble feta on top and bake for 5 more minutes until feta is golden.
6. Serve topped with fresh herbs, pickled onions and a drizzle of olive oil.
7. Enjoy!
